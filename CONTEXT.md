# P2B (Policy-to-Business) Context

P2B is an AI-native platform helping startups, FDI enterprises, and SMEs in Vietnam discover, evaluate, and access government incentives, policy support, and funding programs.

## Language

**P2B**:
The name of the platform.
_Avoid_: Policy Assistant, Policy & Grant Navigator

**Company Passport**:
A structured, audited profile of a business containing consolidated facts (revenue, industry, location, capital, R&D spend, documents) gathered by AI from uploads and public websites.
_Avoid_: Client Profile, Tenant Profile

**Policy**:
A government incentive, grant, tax break, or support program that a Company Passport is matched against.
_Avoid_: Law, regulation, decree

**Legal Document**:
Official legal texts (laws, decrees, circulars) from portals like `vbpl.vn` or `nic.gov.vn` that define Policies.
_Avoid_: Policy paper

**Eligibility Verification**:
The process of evaluating a Company Passport against Policy criteria, classifying each condition into `MET` (đã đạt), `NOT_MET` (chưa đạt), or `MISSING_INFO` (thiếu thông tin), with evidence citations and confidence levels.
_Avoid_: Profile matching check

**Draft**:
An auto-populated registration template or document (typically `.docx`) prepared for a Company, awaiting human approval.
_Avoid_: Output file

**Staging**:
The Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) review dashboard where users audit/edit Drafts and correct Company Passports before final export.
_Avoid_: Review panel, admin check
